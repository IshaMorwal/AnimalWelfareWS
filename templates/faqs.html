FAQs

1. How can I make a donation?

You can make a donation by visiting our donation page, selecting an amount, and completing the payment process. We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and PayPal.

2. Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to Worldwide Veterinary Service (WVS) are tax-deductible in many regions. Please consult your local tax authority for specific details.

3. How will my donation be used?

Your donation will support various programs, including medical care, food supplies, shelter construction, and spay/neuter initiatives for animals in need.

4. Can I cancel my regular donation?

Yes, you can cancel your regular donation at any time by contacting us at support@wvs.org.

5. How can I contact WVS?

You can reach us via email at support@wvs.org or through our Contact Us page.
